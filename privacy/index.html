Privacy & Transparency

Privacy Policy

This website is designed to be lightweight, privacy-friendly and transparent. No unnecessary personal data is collected and no advertising or tracking cookies are used.

1. Data Collection

This website does not actively collect personal data through forms, accounts or tracking systems. Basic technical information may be processed automatically by the hosting provider, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Visited pages
  • Date and time of requests

This information is only used for security, performance and technical troubleshooting purposes.

2. Cookies

This website does not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

If functional cookies are ever used in the future, they will only be used to improve the website experience, for example remembering interface preferences.

3. Third-Party Services

This website may contain links to external websites or services. Visiting external websites is subject to their own privacy policies.

At the time of writing, no external advertising or visitor tracking services are used.

4. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are taken to keep the website secure and protected against unauthorised access. However, no online service can ever be guaranteed to be completely secure.
